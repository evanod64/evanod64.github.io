WIP Zine

Designer / Leads Team

2 designers. 2 copywriters. 2 producers. 2 QAs. 26 POV's.

Entirely curated, edited and designed by Trailheaders at Critical Mass to celebrate the successes (and failures) of Trailhead alumni. The zine is a representation of the talent and opportunities within the program.

We created a a time capsule of our experiences as fresh talent within the advertising industry. Paired with our personal views on Artificial Intelligence, we tried, tested, (failed), and explored the merging of advertising, marketing, creativity and A.I.
